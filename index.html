How to Play

🎮 Controls

W/S or ↑/↓ — Accelerate / Brake

A/D or ←/→ — Steer

E — Interact with buildings (park in lot first)

F — Open ride-hailing phone app

H — Horn   M — Minimap   N — Sound

ESC — Pause & Upgrades

🧭 Navigation & Camera

G — Open "Take me to..." menu (gas station, mechanic, home, etc.)

C — Center camera on your taxi

A green arrow on screen will guide you to your destination!

🚕 How to Play

Drive around the city and pick up passengers on the sidewalk. Deliver them and earn fares!

Park in parking lots (marked P) next to buildings, then press E to refuel ⛽, repair 🔧, or rest 🏡.

⭐ VIP passengers pay 3.5x more! 🧳 Some have luggage (short loading wait, bigger tip).

Weather changes dynamically — rain and night increase demand and fares!

🏆 Tips

• Press G when fuel is low — navigate to the nearest gas station

• Higher star rating = bigger tips from passengers

• Check the phone app (F) for bonus ride orders

• Watch for city events — surge pricing means big money!

• Upgrade your taxi from the pause menu (ESC) for better performance

• Buy new cars at home 🏡 — each has unique stats and advantages!

• Watch for speed limits (120 km/h city, 80 km/h near schools) and red lights 🚦
