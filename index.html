🚕 TAXI DRIVER

City Hustle

How to Play

🎮 Controls

W/S - Accelerate / Brake   A/D - Steer

SPACE - Pick up / Drop off passenger

E - Interact (park in the lot first!)

F - Open ride-hailing app (accept orders)

1 - Navigate to Home   2 - Navigate to Gas Station

3 - Navigate to Mechanic   4 - Navigate to Hospital

0 - Clear navigation

H - Horn   M - Minimap   N - Sound

T - Emergency tow   ESC - Pause

🚕 Gameplay

Drive around the city, pick up passengers waiting on the sidewalk (marked with 🧍), and deliver them to their destinations (marked with 📍).

Earn money for each fare. Park in the parking lot (marked P) next to buildings to refuel ⛽, repair 🔧, or rest at home 🏡.

⚠️ Watch Out For

Speed cameras, rival taxis, thieves disguised as passengers, and random accidents! Keep your car maintained as mileage adds up.

🏆 Goal

Earn as much money as possible! Upgrade your taxi, survive the city, and become the ultimate taxi driver.
